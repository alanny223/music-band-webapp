WHO ARE WE?

The Benites Ministries International is a ministry serving praise and worship unto the Lord. Our mission is to touch hearts, uplift souls and create moments of spiritual communion through the power of music.

The Benites Ministries is subdivided into two branches:

  • The Benites Voices

    The worship team accompanying this ministry, comprised of young authors, composers, musicians, publishers, producers, arrangers, and artists.

  • The Benites Universal Record

    Our dedicated music production label, bringing worship to life through professional recording and production.
