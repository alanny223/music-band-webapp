Our Journey

Revelation

The ministry had been revealed to the founder in a dream where she saw "THE BENITES" written on the sky.

2021

Creation and Launching

THE BENITES MINISTRIES INT'L is Founded on January 21, 2021, in Houston, Texas by the singer and servant of God, Jerusalem Angel.

2022

Relocation

THE BENITES MINISTRIES INTERNATIONAL was relocated in 2022 to Cameroon, specifically to Douala.

2023

Restructuring and Organization

It is composed of a powerful musical team 'THE BENITES VOICES' and a production label 'THE BENITES UNIVERSAL RECORDS.

Our Vision

• To spread the good gospel of salvation through praise and worship to the Lord.

• Perform songs with an evangelistic reach to capture souls through the messages and the smooth melodies of their songs.

• Exalt the love of God.

• Contribute to developing faith, hope, and charity through sharing and prayer.

• Lead the community in better praising and praying to God through song.


Founder and Visionary

Meli Angel Jerusalem

Cameroonian, originally from the Southern region (French-speaking area). Orphaned by both parents, born on September 13, 1963, in Douala, Cameroon. Living in the United States since February 11, 2012, in the state of Texas and the city of Houston. Singer and servant of the Lord; Founder and visionary of The Benites Ministries International; CEO of the gospel music production label The Benites Universal Records (BUR).

I have been involved in gospel music since I was very young. My starting point was in the great classical choir of EPC Manoah. Seven years later, I joined Group K, where I wrote two songs, including the hit 'Je suis libre' and 'Il est Notre Dieu'. A few years later, I journeyed through several revival church choirs, including Winners Chapel Choir, Love Chapel Choir, and La Voix de la Compassion, which I founded.

Upon arriving in the United States, I joined The Levites Choir, Royal Voices, and Graceland Harvest Choir, where I served as a leader. I left this church in 2020 and founded The Benites Ministries International in January 2021. Until now, I have operated in the shadows after several unsuccessful attempts to emerge. I write and compose a lot in French, English, and Bulu. My greatest dream is to become known to the general public by expressing my talent on a global scale.
